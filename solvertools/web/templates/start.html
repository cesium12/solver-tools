This page takes in natural-looking clues, and outputs what it thinks are the best answers that fit your constraints.

You can specify a length or a regular expression to match. Separating words by semicolons mean you want to match almost all of them, not just one.

Examples:

Marsupial → WOMBATS, OPOSSUMRAT, POUCHEDMOLE... (plus lots of extra word information)
Assertion of the falsity of a given proposition (8) → DISPROOF...
US President /.A....../ → VANBUREN, HARRISON, GARFIELD...
/.A.B.C../ → BARBECUE, BARBICAN, NAMBUCCA...
black; union; hi → JACK...
up; down; strange; charm; top; bottom → QUARK, QUARKS, ...
